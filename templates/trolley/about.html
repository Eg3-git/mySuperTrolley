Online shopping has seen a sharp rise in recent years. More people are finding it as a more convenient and attractive option, especially after the uncertainty caused by COVID-19. There are many choices when it comes to picking a place to shop, and it can be tiresome to shop with multiple providers and inconvenient when expecting several deliveries. While there do exist websites containing many big brands, nothing currently exists for more retailer-specific items.

Enter MegaTrolley, an easy-to-use web application which takes in hundreds of products from several retailers for you to choose from, all in one place. While currently small, it has a lot of potential for future expansion.

Thank you for using MegaTrolley!
